Why do I need a pass?

The Interactive Lounge is a space assembled as part of a research study. By using the space, youâ€™ll become a study participant. Your interaction with the space will be remotelly observed and taken notes on (no video recording involved). Therefore, before admiting you into The Interactive Lounge, we need to ensure that you understand the terms and conditions of the study.We also need your informed and expressed consent to participate in the study.

Once that is cleared, we will also ask you to fill a brief questionnaire before you enter the space and after you leave it (not mandatory, but much appreciated).

When we have your consent, we will give you an entry pass code for the day you selected. We will also show you all the fun instructions for how to interact with the lounge that day.
